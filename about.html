About Discover Eswatini

Your trusted local partner for authentic African travel experiences in the Kingdom of Eswatini

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Discover Eswatini was born from a passion to share the authentic beauty and rich cultural heritage of the Kingdom of Eswatini with the world. What started as a small family business has grown into one of Eswatini's most trusted travel companies, while maintaining our commitment to personalized service and genuine cultural experiences.

As proud locals, we understand that Eswatini is more than just a destination – it's a living, breathing kingdom with traditions that span centuries, wildlife that roams freely, and people whose warmth and hospitality are legendary. Our mission is to create meaningful connections between our guests and the heart of Africa.

Our Mission

To provide authentic, sustainable, and transformative travel experiences that celebrate Eswatini's natural beauty, cultural heritage, and warm hospitality while contributing positively to local communities and conservation efforts.

Our Commitments

Environmental Conservation

We partner with local conservation organizations and follow sustainable tourism practices that protect Eswatini's pristine natural environment.

Community Development

A portion of our proceeds goes directly to local community projects, supporting education, healthcare, and cultural preservation initiatives.

Cultural Preservation

We work closely with traditional leaders and cultural custodians to ensure authentic experiences that respect and preserve Swazi heritage.
